reading SURFACE geometries that were converted from XTF SURFACE to
ITF AREA format during the reverse transform.

requires valid polygon topology (closed rings, proper nesting).
The SURFACE→AREA adapter produces geometries that are structurally
valid for writing but fail the reader's polygon validation.
